## The Synergy: Why Combine Them?

Ayurveda dictates that honey and ghee should never be mixed in equal proportions by weight, as they become toxic when perfectly balanced. Panchamrit carefully sidesteps this by using specific ratios—typically vastly more milk and yogurt, with smaller, unequal amounts of ghee and honey.

When these five ingredients are combined exactly right, they undergo a subtle biochemical reaction. The enzymes in the raw honey and yogurt begin to pre-digest the lactose and casein in the A2 milk. The butyric acid in the Bilona ghee emulsifies the entire mixture, creating a stable, hyper-absorbable elixir.
